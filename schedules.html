Invitational and International Finals Schedule

As forms for invitationals and International Finals become available we will update them here.

East Central Bible Quiz Invitational

Saturday October 24 @ 9:30 AM. Hosted by Grace Community Church in Lima, Ohio.
Material: Matthew 1 - 6 covered evenly. We use the Berean Study Bible.

The East Central Quiz Invitational is open to adults, alumni, families, and teens. It is a one day invitational as there is no housing available. The cost is $30 per team or $5 per individual. If you want to quiz you can come as an individual and a team will be found for you to quiz with. Team awards are giver for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place. Other awards are given for "Poetry Slam Proclamation Awards." You can email Jeff Smith if you have any questions.

Central Bible Quiz Invitational

Saturday November 21 @ 9:00 AM. Hosted by Hope Missionary Church in Bluffton, Indiana.
Material: Matthew 1 - 7, 12 - 13 covered evenly. We use the Berean Study Bible.

The Central Quiz Invitational is only going to have one division so please combine your teams to include rookie and experienced quizzers. It is a one day invitational as there is no housing available. The cost is $30 but goes to $35 if postmarked/emailed after November 7. Individuals can combine to form a team but team stacking to win the invitational will not be allowed. Please be sure read the form carefully as there are some recommendations as to masks. You can email Jennifer Littleford if you have any questions.
